FAQ

Good questions to ask before you start.

Can I cancel any time?+
Monthly plans can be cancelled any time. The one-time setup fee isn't refundable once your store has gone live.
Who owns my store data?+
Your products, orders, and customer data are yours. We host and run the platform for you.
Do my customers really see Bangla?+
Yes — shoppers can switch between Bangla and English on your storefront. Your admin dashboard stays in English for your team.
How do I pay?+
bKash, Nagad, or bank transfer — whichever's easiest for you.
How fast can I actually go live?+
Typically 3–7 days after we have your branding and product details — faster if you move quickly on your side.
